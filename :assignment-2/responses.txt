### How does data travel on the internet?
* data travels through the internet on things called packages, where the image or whatnot is broken up into several pieces and reconnects on the other side to form a whole image again.

### What is an IP address and DNS?
* An IP address is a string of numbers broken up with dots that identify every computer and device.
* A DNS is basically a phone book of these IP addresses.

### What is a cookie?
* A cookie is data stored in the users browser for a particular site. They are used to remember information about the user such as login information and shopping cart information.
